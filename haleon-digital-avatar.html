01 — Problem

The Challenge

Retail health advisors can't scale. Shoppers need consistent, 24/7 product guidance — and static posters can't do that.

⚠️

No Consistent Guidance

Shoppers asking about Sensodyne, Panadol, or Voltaren get inconsistent advice depending on which advisor is available — or none at all.

🕐

No 24/7 Availability

Human pharmacists and health advisors aren't always present, especially in large retail stores. The gap is costly and the need is real.

🌳

Complex Symptom Trees

Oral care, cold & flu, and migraine each have multi-step diagnostic flows — too complex to poster-ize, too nuanced for a simple FAQ.

Josephine solves all three — a fully interactive, voice-activated digital health avatar that scales infinitely, never sleeps, and guides every shopper to the right Haleon product.
02 — Solution

Meet Digital Avatar

A real-time Unity3D avatar powered by voice and intent matching — six core systems working as one.

— 01

Voice Input

Listens via Windows Speech API with 4 mic modes: Off, Always-On, Push-to-Talk, and Voice Activation with adaptive noise thresholds.

WindowsSpeechToText.cs
— 02

Intent Matching

Keyword scoring detects Oral Care / Cold & Flu / Migraine from natural speech with tie-breaking logic for ambiguous inputs.

IntentMatcher.cs
— 03

Guided Flows

Multi-step yes/no question trees guide users through up to 10 Q&A steps per health domain to the right Haleon product recommendation.

NPCResponseController.cs
— 04

Live Subtitles

Animated typed subtitles sync to audio with fade in/out transitions. Letter mode or word mode, regex-split per sentence.

SubtitleUI.cs
— 05

Product Popups

Contextual product images (Sensodyne, Panadol, Voltaren) appear at the right moment in each flow via PopupImageUI with 4s fade cycle.

PopupImageUI.cs
— 06

Camera System

Cinemachine multi-angle cameras switch for cinematic presentation. Single button cycles through all virtual camera angles.

CinemachineCameraSwitcher.cs

06 — Voice System

Voice Input & Intent Engine

Four microphone modes, adaptive noise calibration, and keyword scoring with tie-breaking logic.

🎙 Mic Modes

⬛
Off
Microphone fully disabled. No processing.
🔴
Always On
Continuous dictation — recognizer always running. Best for kiosk environments.
🔘
Push to Talk
Hold button → mic activates. Release → stops with 0.5s buffer for result settling.
🌊
Voice Activation
Adaptive noise threshold. Speech triggers → silence stops listening. 2s calibration sample.
Key Implementation
→ 0.15s send delay for result settling
→ Adaptive noise floor calibration (2s)
→ Mic device hot-swap via MicDeviceManager
→ Auto-refreshes mic list every 2 seconds
→ Recognizer auto-restarts on error
