01 — Problem

The Challenge

Retail health advisors can't scale. Shoppers need consistent, 24/7 guidance — static posters can't deliver it.

⚠️

No Consistent Guidance

Shoppers asking about Sensodyne, Panadol, or Voltaren get inconsistent advice depending on who's available — or no advice at all.

🕐

No 24/7 Availability

Human pharmacists aren't always present, especially in large retail environments. The gap is costly and the need is constant.

🌳

Complex Symptom Trees

Oral care, cold & flu, and migraine each need multi-step diagnostic flows — too complex for a simple FAQ, too nuanced for a poster.

Digital Avatar solves all three — a fully interactive, voice-activated digital health avatar that scales infinitely, never sleeps, and guides every shopper to the right product.
02 — Solution

Meet Digital Avatar

A real-time Unity3D avatar powered by voice and intent matching — six core systems working as one.

— 01

Voice Input

Listens via Windows Speech API with 4 mic modes: Off, Always-On, Push-to-Talk, and Voice Activation with adaptive noise thresholds.

WindowsSpeechToText.cs
— 02

Intent Matching

Keyword scoring detects Oral Care / Cold & Flu / Migraine from natural speech with tie-breaking logic for ambiguous inputs.

IntentMatcher.cs
— 03

Guided Flows

Multi-step yes/no question trees guide users through up to 10 Q&A steps per health domain to the right Haleon product.

NPCResponseController.cs
— 04

Live Subtitles

Animated typed subtitles sync to audio with fade in/out transitions. Letter mode or word mode, regex-split per sentence.

SubtitleUI.cs
— 05

Product Popups

Contextual product images (Sensodyne, Panadol, Voltaren) appear at the right moment via PopupImageUI with 4s fade cycle.

PopupImageUI.cs
— 06

Cinemachine multi-angle cameras switch for cinematic presentation. Single button cycles through all virtual camera angles.

CinemachineCameraSwitcher.cs

07 — Context

Frequently Asked Questions

Common operational and technical answers for deploying the chatbot in a retail kiosk environment.

01. How does offline mode work?

The entire language matching runs in local keyword arrays via IntentMatcher.cs. Speech-to-text uses the built-in Windows dictation engine locally, removing server latency completely.

02. How accurate is the mic input in loud spaces?

By sampling environmental ambient audio for 2 seconds at start, Josephine sets a tailored volume threshold. This mitigates extraneous chatter in busy shopping aisles.

03. Can we customize the diagnosis paths?

Yes. The scriptable object framework allows non-technical designers to add branches, alter questions, or insert products through an intuitive C# data array configuration.

04. What about other Haleon products?

Additional health domains can easily be included by appending keys in IntentMatcher.cs and connecting new dialogue sequences in NPCResponseController.cs.
